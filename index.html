Our Story

Kyra and AJ were in Mexico with AJ's family. AJ told Kyra they were doing pictures on the beach one evening before dinner. He did this as a guise so she would get all dressed up and feel her prettiest, knowing that he was about to propose! They went down to the beach gift shop before dinner and Kyra almost messed up the timing of AJ's plan as she wouldn't stop shopping! After some time, he finally convinced her to go for a walk on the beach. During the walk, AJ distracted Kyra by pointing to a golf hole on the resort's course that he played earlier that day. When Kyra turned around, AJ was down on one knee with the ring in his hand! Obviously, she said yes, hit him with her sandal, and cried.

Random Thoughts

What is the significance behind the date, November 22?

1. It's Kassidy's great grandmother's birthday!

2. It's the title of a book written by one of Dennies's favorite authors, Stephen King, 11/22/63

3. In the spirit of getting married at a museum, 11/22 is a very significant day in history (albeit a dark one). It marks the anniversary of JFK's assassination, 11/22/63 (also the central theme of Stephen King's book!)

4. Most importantly, we like the numbers!
