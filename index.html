Our Story

How They Met

Dennies and Kassidy met on the tennis court. They were both on the travel team for St. Joe's Club Tennis team. They began seeing each other throughout college, but Dennies then moved to New York for work. They reconnected while he was in the Philadelphia area for work, and soon after to San Francisco; however, infatuated with Kassidy, Dennies wanted to give the relationship a shot even if it meant long distance. When Dennies first asked Kassidy to be his girlfriend, she hit him and said, "What took you so long?!"

The Proposal

Dennies and Kassidy did long distance for three-and-a-half years when eventually Kassidy moved to San Francisco to be with Dennies. While they loved SF, Kassidy absolutely loved Philly and wanted to move back. Dennies - being an amazing boyfriend - happily went along and they did a roadtrip (their second together!) from SF to PHL. Dennies sealed the deal in August 2024 and proposed to Kassidy where it all started for the two: in the city of brotherly love. Dennies proposed in the presence of their two lovely boys, Loki and Oliver, and Kassidy's maid of honor, Lauren, who helped with the proposal. Dennies promised Kassidy two things that day: that he would be faithfully hers forever, and that they would live in Philly (or the greater Philly area) for as long as she wanted. When he proposed, Kassidy said, "Yes" before he could even get down on one knee.

Random Thoughts

What is the significance behind the date, November 22?

1. It's Kassidy's great grandmother's birthday!

2. It's the title of a book written by one of Dennies's favorite authors, Stephen King, 11/22/63

3. In the spirit of getting married at a museum, 11/22 is a very significant day in history (albeit a dark one). It marks the anniversary of JFK's assassination, 11/22/63 (also the central theme of Stephen King's book!)

4. Most importantly, we like the numbers!
