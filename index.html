Our Story

It all began at Saint Joseph's University, where two ambitious double majors, Kassidy and Dennies, crossed paths on the club tennis team. Dennies, the team captain, couldn’t help but notice Kassidy’s charm both on and off the court. Their shared passion for tennis, accounting, and management information systems laid the foundation for a connection that quickly blossomed into something extraordinary.

From study sessions to tennis matches, their bond deepened, and before long, Kassidy and Dennies became inseparable. After graduating magna cum laude together, life had more adventures in store. They navigated nearly six years of love, including 3.5 years of long-distance, living apart yet never far from each other’s hearts. Their journey took them across the country, from Philadelphia to San Francisco, and back again.

Through it all, they grew their little family, adopting two adorable dogs, Oliver and Loki, who quickly became the center of their world. Then, on a sunny August 8th in the city where it all began, Dennies asked Kassidy the question she had been waiting for. Against the backdrop of Philadelphia's familiar streets, he popped the question—and she said yes!

Now, as they prepare to start their next chapter together in the city that witnessed the beginning of their love story, Kassidy and Dennies can’t wait to celebrate their wedding day surrounded by the people who mean the most to them.
